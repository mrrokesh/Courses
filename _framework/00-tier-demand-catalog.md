# Enrollment Guidance by Tier

**Tier 1 — Prioritize for:**
- Placement drives and internship batches
- 6-month and 9-month packages
- Maximum marketing spend and brochure visibility

**Tier 2 — Prioritize for:**
- ECE/EEE/Mechatronics department tie-ups
- Lab/hardware kit bundles
- Industry 4.0 and automation partnerships

**Tier 3 — Prioritize for:**
- Specialized skill add-ons
- Working professionals upskilling
- College elective credit modules (1–3 months)
- **Fashion Technology**, design, and creative industry partnerships
