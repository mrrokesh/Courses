**MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY — Training & Internship Programs**

</div>

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Developers exploring decentralized applications, smart contracts, and Web3 engineering.

**Prerequisites:** JavaScript/Python; basic understanding of databases and APIs.

**Tools:** Ethereum, Solidity, Hardhat/Foundry, MetaMask, Remix, Ethers.js, IPFS, testnets (Sepolia).
