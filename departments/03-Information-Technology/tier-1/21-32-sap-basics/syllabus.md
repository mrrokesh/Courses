**MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY — Training & Internship Programs**

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Business graduates, ERP consultants, and professionals entering SAP-centric enterprises.

**Prerequisites:** Basic business process knowledge (sales, procurement, finance).

**Tools:** SAP S/4HANA or ECC sandbox (IDES), SAP GUI/Fiori, transaction codes (T-codes).

**Certification targets:** SAP TS4C01 / C_TS410 (awareness level, 6-month)
