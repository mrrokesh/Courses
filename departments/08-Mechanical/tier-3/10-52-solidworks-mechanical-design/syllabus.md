**Tier 3 — Specialized | Departments: Mechanical · Mechatronics**

</div>

**Related:** [Track 37 — CAD/CAM](37-cad-cam-mechanical-design.md) · [Track 45 — CATIA](45-catia-mechanical-design.md) · [Track 46 — ANSYS](46-ansys-simulation-analysis.md)

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Mechanical and Mechatronics students targeting design engineer roles in manufacturing, automotive, and product companies.

**Prerequisites:** Engineering drawing and mechanics basics.

**Tools:** SolidWorks (latest), Simulation module, Composer (intro), PDM vault (6-month+).

---

## Outcomes by Duration

| Duration | Hours | Job Readiness | Key Deliverables |
|----------|-------|---------------|------------------|
| **2 Weeks** | 40–50 | SolidWorks literacy | Part + assembly + drawing |
| **1 Month** | 80–100 | Junior designer | Mechanism design project |
| **3 Months** | 250–300 | Design engineer intern | Simulated + documented product |
| **6 Months** | 500–600 | CSWP-ready portfolio | 5+ industry-style projects |

---

## 2-Week Crash Course

| Days | Topics | Deliverable |
|------|--------|-------------|
| D1–2 | Interface, sketch tools, constraints | 5 part models |
| D3–4 | Assembly mates, exploded views | Mechanism assembly |
| D5–6 | Drawings, GD&T intro, BOM | Manufacturing drawing set |
| D7–8 | Simulation: static stress, FOS | FEA report |
| D9–10 | Capstone | **Complete design package** |

---

## 1-Month Foundation

| Week | Topics | Project |
|------|--------|---------|
| W1 | Advanced modeling: sweeps, lofts, surfaces | Complex parts library |
| W2 | Top-down design, configurations, design tables | Parametric product family |
| W3 | Sheet metal, weldments | Fabrication-ready designs |
| W4 | Motion study, rendering, capstone | **Product design project** |

---

## 3-Month Job Ready

Machine elements (gears, shafts, bearings) + assembly design + simulation validation + design portfolio.

---

## 6-Month Professional

CSWP certification prep + surfacing + mold design intro + PLM/PDM workflow + client-style capstone.
