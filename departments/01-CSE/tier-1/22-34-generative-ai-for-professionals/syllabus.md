## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Working professionals across all industries wanting practical GenAI skills for career advancement.

**Prerequisites:** None for 2-week/1-month tiers; basic digital literacy sufficient.

**Tools:** ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, Midjourney/DALL-E, ElevenLabs, NotebookLM, Custom GPTs, Zapier, Canva AI.

**Distinction:** Broader than Track 33 (which focuses on prompt templates for business). Track 34 covers **multi-modal GenAI** (text, image, audio, video) and **professional innovation workflows**.
