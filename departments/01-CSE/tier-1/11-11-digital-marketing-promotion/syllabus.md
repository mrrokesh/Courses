## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Aspiring digital marketers, social media managers, freelancers, and entrepreneurs.

**Prerequisites:** Basic computer literacy; familiarity with social media platforms helpful.

**Tools & lab environment:** Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console, Google Ads (simulated/live), Meta Business Suite, SEMrush/Ahrefs (trial), HubSpot/Mailchimp, Canva, WordPress (landing pages).

**Certification targets (6-month):** Google Ads Search, Meta Blueprint, HubSpot Email, Google Analytics Certification

## 2-Week Crash Course

**Deliverable:** Digital marketing playbook (mini) | **Level:** L1

| Days | Topics | Deliverable | Self-Check |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| D1–2 | Marketing fundamentals, customer journey, personas | Persona document | Define TOFU/MOFU/BOFU |
| D3–4 | SEO: keywords, on-page, technical audit | SEO audit report | Title tag vs meta description |
| D5–6 | Google Ads: search campaigns, keyword bidding | Campaign plan | Quality Score factors |

## 1-Month Foundation

**Capstone:** Complete marketing playbook | **Level:** L2

| Week | Topics | Project |
|------|--------|---------|
| W1 | Strategy, SEO fundamentals + audit | SEO audit for real brand |
| W2 | Google Ads (Search + Display) | Live/simulated ad campaign |
| W3 | Social media + content marketing | 30-day content plan |
| W4 | Email marketing, GA4, capstone | **Complete marketing playbook** |

**W4 acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] Persona + customer journey map
- [ ] SEO audit with 10+ actionable recommendations
- [ ] Ad campaign structure with keyword list and ad copy
- [ ] 30-day content calendar with post mockups
- [ ] Email sequence (3+ emails) drafted

| Month | Topics | Projects |

## Appendix: Mentor Notes

**Common pitfalls:** Vanity metrics without conversions; no UTM tracking; generic ad copy; ignoring mobile landing page experience.

**Stretch goals:** Run live campaign with small budget (₹500–1000); publish guest blog post for SEO backlink.
