**MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY — Training & Internship Programs**

</div>

**Related:** [Track 16 — Data Analytics](16-data-analytics.md) | [Track 1 — Full Stack](01-full-stack-development.md)

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** CRM administrators, business analysts, and developers entering the Salesforce ecosystem.

**Prerequisites:** Basic business process understanding; no coding for admin tiers.

**Tools:** Salesforce Trailhead, Developer Edition org, Flow Builder, Process Builder, SOQL, Apex (6-month+), Lightning Web Components (9-month).

**Certification targets:** Salesforce Administrator (6-month), Platform Developer I (9-month)
