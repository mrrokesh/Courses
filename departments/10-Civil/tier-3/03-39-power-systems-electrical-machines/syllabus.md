# Track 39: Power Systems & Electrical Machines

**Department:** EEE | **MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY**

</div>

> **Skill path:** DC/AC Machines → Transformers → Transmission → Load Flow → Protection → MATLAB Simulation → Industrial Drives

**Related:** [Track 25 — PLC & Industrial Automation](25-plc-industrial-automation.md) | [Track 38 — MATLAB & Simulink](38-matlab-simulink-engineering.md)

---

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** EEE students targeting power utilities, industrial drives, and electrical design roles.

**Prerequisites:** Basic circuit theory and electromagnetism.

**Tools:** MATLAB/Simulink, ETAP (or PowerWorld intro), multimeter/motor lab kits, simulation software.

---

| Duration | Hours | Job Readiness | Key Deliverables |
|----------|-------|---------------|------------------|

---

## 2-Week Crash Course

| Days | Topics | Deliverable |
|------|--------|-------------|
| D1–2 | DC machines: construction, characteristics, speed control | DC motor lab/sim report |
| D3–4 | Transformers: equivalent circuit, efficiency, regulation | Transformer calculation sheet |
| D5–6 | 3-phase induction motor, starting methods | Motor performance curves |
| D7–8 | Power generation, transmission basics, per-unit system | Single-line diagram |
| D9–10 | MATLAB simulation, capstone | **Machine simulation project** |
