## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Data analysts and BI professionals preferring Tableau for visualization and storytelling.

**Prerequisites:** Basic Excel; SQL helpful for 1-month+ tiers.

**Tools:** Tableau Desktop/Public, Tableau Prep, calculated fields, parameters, dashboards.

**Certification targets:** Tableau Desktop Specialist / Certified Data Analyst (6-month)
