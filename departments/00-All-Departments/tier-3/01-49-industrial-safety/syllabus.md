# Track 49: Industrial Safety

**Tier 3 — Specialized | Departments: All Engineering Branches**

</div>

> **Skill path:** Safety Regulations → Hazard Identification → Risk Assessment → PPE → LOTO → Fire Safety → OSHA/Factory Act → Safety Audits

**Related:** [Track 25 — PLC & Industrial Automation](25-plc-industrial-automation.md) · [Department Map](../00-department-course-map.md)

---

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** All engineering students and shop-floor professionals; mandatory add-on for manufacturing, EEE, Mechatronics, and Civil site roles.

**Prerequisites:** None; applicable across all departments.

**Tools:** HIRA templates, JSA forms, safety audit checklists, case study library, PPE demonstration kits.

**Certification targets:** OSHA 30-hour (awareness), NEBOSH IGC intro (6-month, optional)

---

| Duration | Hours | Job Readiness | Key Deliverables |
|----------|-------|---------------|------------------|
| **2 Weeks** | 40–50 | Safety awareness | HIRA for sample workplace |

| **6 Months** | 500–600 | EHS coordinator track | Industry safety capstone |

## 2-Week Crash Course

| Days | Topics | Deliverable |
|------|--------|-------------|
| D1–2 | Safety culture, Factory Act/OSHA basics, rights/duties | Safety policy draft |
| D3–4 | Hazard identification, HIRA, JSA | HIRA document |
| D5–6 | PPE, machine guarding, electrical safety | PPE assessment checklist |
| D7–8 | Fire safety, emergency response, first aid basics | Emergency plan |
| D9–10 | Capstone | **Workplace safety audit report** |

---

## 1-Month Foundation

| Week | Topics | Project |
|------|--------|---------|
| W1 | LOTO, confined spaces, hot work permits | Permit system documentation |
| W2 | Chemical safety, MSDS, spill response | Chemical handling SOP |
| W3 | Ergonomics, noise, ventilation, lighting | Workplace assessment report |
| W4 | Incident investigation, near-miss reporting, capstone | **Safety management system binder** |

---

## Department-Specific Modules (Elective Deep-Dive)

| Department | Focus Module |
|------------|--------------|
| **EEE / Mechatronics** | Electrical arc flash, LOTO for drives, PLC safety circuits |
| **Mechanical / CNC** | Machine guarding, CNC safety, material handling |
| **Civil** | Construction site safety, scaffolding, excavation, fall protection |
| **ECE / IoT** | Lab electrical safety, battery storage safety |
| **Chemical** | Process safety, HAZOP intro (stretch) |

---

## 3–6 Month Progression

Safety training delivery + mock audit + ISO 45001 awareness + capstone safety improvement project at partner industry (6-month).

---

## Assessment Rubric

Weekly Quiz 20% | Audit/checklist labs 40% | Project 30% | Portfolio 10%

## Appendix: Mentor Notes

**Cross-department Tier 3 course.** Recommend as add-on to Tier 2 manufacturing tracks (PLC, Mechatronics, CNC). Include at least one site visit or simulated audit.
