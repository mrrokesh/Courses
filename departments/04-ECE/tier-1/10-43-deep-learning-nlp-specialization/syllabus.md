## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** AI & ML department students specializing in deep learning and natural language processing beyond foundational ML.

**Prerequisites:** Python, basic ML ([Track 5](05-ai-ml-data-science.md) M1–M3 or equivalent).

**Tools:** PyTorch, TensorFlow/Keras, Hugging Face Transformers, Weights & Biases, Jupyter, GPU (Colab/local).

## Outcomes by Duration

| Duration | Hours | Job Readiness | Key Deliverables |
|----------|-------|---------------|------------------|
| **2 Weeks** | 40–50 | DL awareness | Trained CNN + NLP classifier |
| **1 Month** | 80–100 | Junior DL engineer | Fine-tuned transformer model |
| **3 Months** | 250–300 | Internship-ready | Production NLP/CV capstone |

---

## 2-Week Crash Course

| Days | Topics | Deliverable |
|------|--------|-------------|
| D1–2 | Neural nets, backprop, PyTorch tensors | MNIST classifier |
| D3–4 | CNNs: Conv layers, pooling, architectures | Image classifier |
| D5–6 | RNNs, LSTM, text preprocessing | Sentiment classifier |
| D7–8 | Transformers, attention, Hugging Face | BERT fine-tune demo |
| D9–10 | Capstone | **NLP or CV deep learning project** |

---

## 1-Month Foundation

| Week | Topics | Project |
|------|--------|---------|
| W1 | PyTorch deep dive, training loops, GPU | Custom CNN from scratch |
| W2 | Transfer learning, ResNet, EfficientNet | Image classification app |
| W3 | Transformers, tokenization, fine-tuning | Domain-specific text classifier |
| W4 | Model evaluation, deployment (ONNX/Flask) | **Deployed DL model API** |

## 3-Month Job Ready

| Month | Topics | Projects |

M1–M3 + LLM fine-tuning (LoRA) + multi-modal models + W&B monitoring + 3 deployed models.

## 9-Month Advanced
