**MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY — Training & Internship Programs**

</div>

**Related:** [Track 23 — ROS2](23-ros2.md) | [Track 26 — Computer Vision](26-computer-vision.md)

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Aerospace, robotics, and CS students interested in UAV software and autonomous flight.

**Prerequisites:** Python; basic physics; [ROS2](23-ros2.md) helpful for 3-month+ tiers.

**Tools:** PX4 SITL / ArduPilot SITL, Gazebo, QGroundControl, MAVSDK-Python, Mission Planner, drone simulator.

**Safety:** All flight labs in simulation until 6-month+ tier with certified outdoor flying authorization.

## 2-Week Crash Course

| Days | Topics | Deliverable |
|------|--------|-------------|
| D1–2 | Drone anatomy, flight dynamics, regulations | Safety + regulations quiz |
