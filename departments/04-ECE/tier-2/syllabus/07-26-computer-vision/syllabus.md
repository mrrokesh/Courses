## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** AI/ML engineers, robotics developers, and developers building visual intelligence systems.

**Prerequisites:** Python; basic linear algebra helpful; [Track 5](05-ai-ml-data-science.md) ML basics for 3-month+ tiers.

**Tools:** Python, OpenCV, NumPy, PyTorch/TensorFlow, YOLO, MediaPipe, Roboflow, Jupyter, ONNX/TensorRT (6-month+).

|------|--------|-------------|
