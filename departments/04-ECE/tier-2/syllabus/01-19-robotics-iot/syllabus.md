## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Engineering students, hobbyists, and professionals entering IoT and robotics fields.

**Prerequisites:** Basic physics/electronics helpful; programming basics (Python/C++) for 1-month+ tiers.

**Tools:** Arduino/Raspberry Pi, sensors (DHT, ultrasonic, IR), MQTT, Node-RED, Blynk/ThingSpeak, breadboards, multimeter.

**Hardware kit required:** Arduino Uno/Nano + sensor kit (institution-provided or student-purchased)
