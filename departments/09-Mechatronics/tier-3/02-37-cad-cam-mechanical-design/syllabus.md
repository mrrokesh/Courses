# Track 37: CAD/CAM & Mechanical Design

**Department:** Mechanical Engineering / Mechatronics | **MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY**

</div>

> **Skill path:** Engineering Drawing → SolidWorks/Fusion 360 → Assembly → Simulation → Drafting → CAM Toolpaths

**Related:** [Track 42 — Mechatronics Integration](42-mechatronics-systems-integration.md) | [Department Map](../00-department-course-map.md)

---

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Mechanical and mechatronics students targeting design engineer and manufacturing roles.

**Prerequisites:** Basic engineering mechanics and drawing standards.

**Tools:** SolidWorks (or Fusion 360), ANSYS Mechanical (intro), CAM module, 3D printing lab (optional).

---

## Outcomes by Duration

| Duration | Hours | Job Readiness | Key Deliverables |
|----------|-------|---------------|------------------|
| **2 Weeks** | 40–50 | 3D modeling awareness | Part + assembly model |
| **1 Month** | 80–100 | Junior design engineer | Mechanism assembly + drawings |
| **3 Months** | 250–300 | Internship-ready | Designed + simulated component |
| **6 Months** | 500–600 | Design engineer ready | Portfolio of 5+ mechanical designs |

---

## 2-Week Crash Course

| Days | Topics | Deliverable |
|------|--------|-------------|
| D1–2 | Sketch constraints, extrude, revolve, patterns | 5 part models |
| D3–4 | Assembly mates, sub-assemblies, exploded views | Mechanism assembly |
| D5–6 | Engineering drawings: GD&T intro, BOM | Manufacturing drawing set |
| D7–8 | Simulation: static stress, factor of safety | FEA report (simple part) |
| D9–10 | CAM intro, capstone | **Designed part + drawing + sim report** |

---

## 1-Month Foundation

| Week | Topics | Project |
|------|--------|---------|
| W1 | Advanced modeling: sweeps, lofts, surfaces | Complex part library |
| W2 | Top-down vs bottom-up assembly design | Gear/ linkage mechanism |
| W3 | Sheet metal, weldments, configurations | Fabrication-ready design |
| W4 | Simulation + technical drawings | **Complete design project** |

---

## 3-Month Job Ready

| Month | Topics | Projects |
|-------|--------|----------|
| M1 | Machine elements design (shafts, bearings, gears) | Gearbox assembly |
| M2 | FEA: thermal, modal analysis intro | Optimized bracket design |
| M3 | CAM toolpaths, capstone | **Design-to-manufacture capstone** |

---

## 6-Month Professional

M1–M3 + advanced simulation + design portfolio + industry standard parts ( fasteners library) + 3D print prototype.

---

## Assessment Rubric

Weekly Quiz 20% | CAD labs 40% | Project 30% | Portfolio 10%
