**Tier 3 — Specialized | Departments: Mechanical · Civil · Mechatronics · EEE**

</div>

**Related:** [Track 52 — SolidWorks](52-solidworks-mechanical-design.md) · [Track 36 — BIM/Structural](36-bim-structural-engineering.md)

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Mechanical, Civil (structural), Mechatronics, and EEE students needing FEA/CFD simulation skills for design validation.

**Prerequisites:** Strength of materials, basic CAD ([Track 52](52-solidworks-mechanical-design.md) or [35 AutoCAD](35-autocad-civil-design.md)).

**Tools:** ANSYS Mechanical, ANSYS Fluent (intro), SpaceClaim/DesignModeler, student license.

## 2-Week Crash Course

| Days | Topics | Deliverable |
|------|--------|-------------|
| D1–2 | FEA theory, meshing, boundary conditions | Meshed simple part |
| D3–4 | Static structural analysis, stress/strain | FEA report with FOS |
| D5–6 | Modal analysis, natural frequencies | Vibration report |
| D7–8 | Thermal analysis intro | Heat transfer simulation |
| D9–10 | Capstone | **Simulation validation report** |

| Week | Topics | Project |
