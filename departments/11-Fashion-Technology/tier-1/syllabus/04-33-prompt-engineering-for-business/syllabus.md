**MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY — Training & Internship Programs**

## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Managers, marketers, HR professionals, educators, and non-technical staff using AI for daily work.

**Prerequisites:** None; basic computer and email skills sufficient. **No coding required.**

**Tools:** ChatGPT, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, Google Gemini, Notion AI, Canva AI, no-code automation (Zapier/n8n intro).

**Distinction from Track 17:** Track 33 is **no-code, business-focused**. Track 17 is **developer/API-focused**.

|------|--------|-------------|
