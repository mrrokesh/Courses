## Track Overview

**Who this is for:** Office administrators, accounting assistants, business students, entrepreneurs, and Tally operators.

**Prerequisites:** Basic computer literacy; typing skills helpful.

**Tools & lab environment:** Microsoft Office 365 or Office 2021 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), Tally Prime (Educational/License), sample business datasets, GST portal demo access.

**Certification targets:** Tally Prime certification (optional); Microsoft Office Specialist (optional)

## 2-Week Crash Course

**Deliverable:** Complete business report package | **Level:** L1

| Days | Topics | Practical Output | Checklist |
|------|--------|------------------|-----------|
| D1–2 | MS Word: formatting, styles, templates, mail merge | Business letter + report | [ ] Styles applied consistently |
| D3–4 | MS Excel: formulas, pivot tables, charts | Sales dashboard | [ ] SUMIF/VLOOKUP working |
| D5–6 | MS PowerPoint: professional presentations | Company pitch deck | [ ] Master slide template used |
| D7–8 | Tally Prime: company creation, vouchers, GST basics | Sample company accounts | [ ] 5 voucher types entered |
| D9–10 | Data entry, business documentation | **Complete business report package** | [ ] All files in organized folder |

**Output checklist (not coding labs):**
- [ ] Word report: 5+ pages, table of contents, headers/footers
- [ ] Excel dashboard: 3+ charts, pivot table, conditional formatting
- [ ] PowerPoint: 10+ slides with consistent branding
- [ ] Tally: Company created with 10+ transactions

## Appendix: Mentor Notes

**Common pitfalls:** Unbalanced Tally vouchers; Excel formula errors in financial sheets; inconsistent date formats; missing GST fields.

**Hardware:** Windows PC recommended for Tally Prime; Office 365 license required.
