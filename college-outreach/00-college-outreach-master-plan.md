# MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY

**College Outreach Master Plan**

*Workshops · Seminars · Bootcamps · MoU Partnerships*

</div>

> **Goal:** Land college events → collect leads → convert students into 2-week to 9-month training batches.  
> **Tagline:** Learn → Build → Deploy → Get Hired

---

## Event Types

| Type | Duration | Best For | Outcome |
|------|----------|----------|---------|
| Expert Talk / Guest Lecture | 1–2 hrs | HOD approval | Brand awareness |
| Seminar | 3–4 hrs | Department-wide | Lead collection |
| Hands-on Workshop | 1 day | Lab departments | Strong conversion |
| Bootcamp | 2–3 days | Vacation / weekend | Paid signup |
| Skill Sprint | 1 week | NAAC / elective tie-up | MoU discussion |
| Placement Prep Day | 1 day | Final year | Batch enrollment |
| FDP (Faculty) | 2 days | Faculty buy-in | Partnership |

**First visit formula:** Free Seminar → 1-Day Workshop → 3–6 Month Batch

---

## 90-Day Plan

### Weeks 1–2 — Prepare
- Print department posters & brochures (this kit)
- Prepare live demo per department
- Google Form: Name, Dept, Year, WhatsApp, Interest
- WhatsApp list per college

### Weeks 3–6 — Approach (30 colleges)
**Contact order:** TPO → HOD → IQAC → Student club president

**Mix:** 10 engineering · 10 arts/science with IT · 5 fashion · 5 polytechnic

### Weeks 7–10 — Deliver
- 2 events/week (seminar + workshop)
- End with free career assessment signup
- 7-day early-bird discount

### Weeks 11–12 — Convert
| Student | Offer |
|---------|-------|
| High interest | 2-week Skill Sprint |
| Final year | 3-month Job Ready |
| 2nd/3rd year | 6-month Professional |
| Stuck project | Track 12 Project Support |

## Standard 3-Hour Seminar Flow

| Time | Segment |
|------|---------|
| 0:00–0:15 | Intro + industry gap + MR ROKESH overview |
| 0:15–0:45 | Department topic teaching |
| 0:45–1:15 | **Live demo** |
| 1:15–1:30 | Break + QR registration |
| 1:30–2:00 | Career paths + salaries |
| 2:00–2:20 | Success stories / portfolios |
| 2:20–2:45 | Q&A |
| 2:45–3:00 | CTA: career assessment + early-bird offer |

## 30-Second Pitch

> "Sir/Madam, I'm from **MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY**. We run industry training across **53 tracks** — Full Stack, AI, Robotics, EV, Civil BIM, Fashion Technology with CLO3D, and more.
>
> We'd like to conduct a **free [Department Seminar Title]** for your students — hands-on, certificate included, zero cost to the college.
>
> Our philosophy: **Learn → Build → Deploy → Get Hired**. Interested students can join **2-week to 6-month** placement batches after the event.
>
> Can we schedule 2–3 hours this month?"
