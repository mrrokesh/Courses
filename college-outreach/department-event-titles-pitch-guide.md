## Duration Guide

| Format | Duration | What You Deliver | Best Pitch To |
|--------|----------|------------------|---------------|
| **Seminar** | 3 hours | Expert talk + live demo + Q&A + registration | HOD (first contact) |
| **Half Day** | 4 hours | Seminar + guided mini-lab + certificate | HOD + lab coordinator |
| **Full Day** | 6–7 hours | Hands-on workshop + student project start | Department event / club |
| **2-Day Bootcamp** | 12–14 hours | Deep lab + mini capstone + portfolio piece | Vacation / weekend / fest |

**Pricing anchor:** Seminar = FREE (lead gen) · Half Day = free/low · Full Day & Bootcamp = paid/sponsored
