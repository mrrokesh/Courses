## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to collaborate for the promotion of industry-aligned skill development, training, workshops, seminars, internships, and placement-oriented programs for students of the Institution.

---

## 2. Scope of Collaboration

MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY may offer the following to Institution students:

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Seminars & Guest Lectures** | Free awareness sessions (1–3 hours) |
| **Workshops & Bootcamps** | Hands-on skill sessions (1–3 days) |
| **Training Programs** | 2-week to 9-month industry tracks (paid by students) |
| **Project Support** | Final-year and academic project mentoring |
| **Placement Support** | Resume, portfolio, mock interview assistance |
| **Faculty Development** | FDP on industry tools and assessment (optional) |

**Departments covered:** CSE, IT, AI & DS, AI & ML, Cyber Security, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Mechatronics, Civil Engineering, Fashion Technology, and allied branches as mutually agreed.

---

## 3. Responsibilities of the Institution

1. Provide suitable venue (auditorium, seminar hall, or lab) for scheduled events.
2. Provide projector, screen, power supply, and basic seating.
3. Facilitate internal communication to students via notice, email, or department coordinators.
4. Nominate a **Faculty Coordinator** and **TPO liaison** for each activity.
5. Permit distribution of program brochures and registration materials to interested students.
6. For lab-based workshops, provide computer lab access or workshop space as agreed in advance.

---

## 4. Responsibilities of MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY

1. Deliver seminars, workshops, and training as per agreed schedule and syllabus.
2. Provide qualified trainers / industry mentors for scheduled sessions.
3. Issue **participation certificates** for seminar/workshop attendees.
4. Maintain professional conduct and comply with Institution rules on campus.
5. Share registration and attendance summary with the Institution coordinator post-event.
6. Offer preferential enrollment terms for Institution students (as per separate fee schedule).

---

---

## 6. Intellectual Property

- Training materials, syllabi, and course content remain the property of MR ROKESH TECHNOLOGY.
- Student projects created during training belong to students unless otherwise agreed for showcase purposes.
- Neither party may use the other's logo or name in public marketing without written consent.

---

## 7. Duration & Termination

1. This MoU shall remain valid for **one (1) year** from the Effective Date.
2. Either party may terminate with **30 days written notice**.
3. Activities scheduled before termination shall be completed unless both parties agree otherwise.

---

## 8. Non-Exclusivity

This MoU is **non-exclusive**. Both parties may enter similar arrangements with other organizations.

---

## 9. Confidentiality

Both parties shall keep confidential any proprietary student data, fee negotiations, or internal institutional information shared under this MoU, except as required by law.

---

## 10. Dispute Resolution

Disputes shall be resolved through **mutual discussion**. If unresolved, matters shall be referred to courts at **[City, State]**.
